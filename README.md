Excited to share my latest Power BI Project: Online vs Offline Sales Dashboard 

I recently built an interactive dashboard to analyze and compare online and offline sales performance across different dimensions like region, category, time, and payment modes.

 Key Highlights:
 Total Sales: 3108K+ with strong contribution from online channels
 Profit Analysis: Achieved 621K+ total profit with a 20% margin
 Channel Insights: Online sales dominate with ~68% share compared to offline
 Regional Trends: South and East regions lead in overall sales
 Category Analysis: Electronics and Clothing are top-performing categories
 Payment Behavior: Balanced usage across Card, Cash, and UPI

 What I focused on:
 Created dynamic KPIs for Sales, Profit, Orders, and Margin
 Used DAX measures for calculations like Profit Margin & Total Orders
 Built interactive slicers (Region, Month, Sales Channel)
 Designed clean and user-friendly visuals for better storytelling
 Added comparison views to highlight online vs offline trends

 Insights Gained:
Online sales are clearly driving growth, while offline sales still contribute significantly in certain regions and categories. This opens opportunities to optimize channel strategies and improve overall business performance.

 Tools Used: Power BI | DAX | Data Modeling | Data Visualization

This project helped me strengthen my skills in data analysis, dashboard design, and business storytelling.
